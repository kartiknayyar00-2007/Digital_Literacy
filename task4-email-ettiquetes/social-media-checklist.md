# Social Media Do's and Don'ts Checklist

## ✅ Do's (Responsible Social Media Use)

- Do maintain a professional and respectful online presence  
- Do verify information before sharing any content  
- Do use privacy settings to protect your personal information  
- Do think before posting or commenting online  
- Do use social media for learning, networking, and skill development  

---

## ❌ Don'ts (Avoid These Practices)

- Don't share personal details like passwords, address, or phone number  
- Don't post inappropriate, offensive, or harmful content  
- Don't spread fake news or unverified information  
- Don't engage in online arguments or cyberbullying  
- Don't waste excessive time on social media  

---

## 📌 Conclusion

Following these Do’s and Don’ts helps ensure safe, responsible, and productive use of social media as a college student. It also helps in building a positive digital identity and avoiding potential risks online.
