# 🛡️ Stay Safe Online – Prevention Checklist (For College Students in India)

## ✅ Actionable Safety Tips

1. **Use Strong and Unique Passwords**  
   Create passwords with a mix of letters, numbers, and symbols. Avoid using the same password across multiple accounts.

2. **Enable Two-Factor Authentication (2FA)**  
   Always turn on 2FA for email, social media, and banking apps for extra security.

3. **Never Share OTP or UPI PIN** 💳  
   Banks or apps never ask for OTP or PIN. Sharing these can lead to instant financial loss.

4. **Be Careful with UPI “Collect Requests”** 💰  
   Always check before approving. Remember: *receiving money does not require entering your UPI PIN.*

5. **Avoid Clicking on Unknown Links**  
   Do not open suspicious links from SMS, emails, or social media messages.

6. **Keep Apps and Devices Updated**  
   Regular updates fix security bugs and protect against cyber threats.

7. **Use Secure Wi-Fi Networks**  
   Avoid logging into banking apps or sensitive accounts on public Wi-Fi.

8. **Think Before You Share Online**  
   Avoid posting personal details like phone number, address, or ID documents.

9. **Log Out from Shared Devices**  
   Always log out after using computers in libraries or cyber cafes.

## 🚨 Reporting Cybercrime (India)

- 🌐 National Cyber Crime Portal: https://cybercrime.gov.in  
- 📞 Cyber Crime Helpline: **1930**

👉 Report fraud immediately to increase chances of recovering lost money.
